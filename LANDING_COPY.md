## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What does AI Spend Audit do?

AI Spend Audit analyzes your subscriptions to tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, and GitHub Copilot. It estimates monthly and annual savings and recommends better plans or alternative tools.

### 2. How long does the audit take?

Most users complete the form and receive their personalized audit in less than two minutes.

### 3. Which AI tools are supported?

The tool currently supports ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, OpenAI API, and Anthropic API.

### 4. Is my company information public?

No. Public share links intentionally exclude sensitive information such as email address, company name, and role.

### 5. How accurate are the savings estimates?

Savings estimates are based on official vendor pricing and practical optimization heuristics. They are designed to provide realistic directional guidance rather than exact contractual quotes.
