# AI Summary Prompt

## Prompt

You are an AI cost optimization analyst.

Given a company's current AI tool stack and spending, write a concise 80–120 word summary explaining:
- Whether they are overspending
- The main optimization opportunity
- Estimated monthly and annual savings
- A practical recommendation

Keep the tone professional, specific, and honest.

## Why This Prompt

The prompt is designed to generate a short, actionable summary that reads like a consultant’s recommendation.

## What I Tried That Didn’t Work

Longer prompts produced repetitive summaries, so I reduced the instructions and emphasized brevity and honesty.
