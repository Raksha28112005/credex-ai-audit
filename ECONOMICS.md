# Economics

## Value of a Converted Lead

A converted lead is a company that books a Credex consultation and ultimately purchases a credit product. Assume the average startup purchases a $250,000 working capital facility and Credex earns a 2% revenue share or origination fee. That produces $5,000 in revenue per successful customer.

Not every consultation closes. If 20% of consultations convert into funded customers, then each booked consultation is worth:

- $5,000 × 20% = $1,000 expected value

If 10% of completed audits turn into booked consultations, then each completed audit is worth:

- $1,000 × 10% = $100 expected value

This means generating qualified audit completions can be highly valuable even if only a small percentage convert.

## Customer Acquisition Cost by Channel

Based on the GTM plan, paid advertising is not used. The primary cost is founder time.

- Indie Hackers / Hacker News / Reddit: 5 hours of work at an estimated $50/hour = $250. If this generates 100 audits, CAC per audit = $2.50.
- LinkedIn outreach: 10 hours to send personalized messages = $500. If this generates 20 audits, CAC per audit = $25.
- X (Twitter) thread and referral sharing: 3 hours = $150. If this generates 50 audits, CAC per audit = $3.

A blended estimate across channels is approximately $5 per completed audit.

## Conversion Rates Required for Profitability

Assume:

- Audit completion → consultation booked: 10%
- Consultation booked → funded credit customer: 20%
- Revenue per funded customer: $5,000

Expected revenue per audit:

- 10% × 20% × $5,000 = $100

If CAC per audit is $5, gross profit per audit is approximately $95. This is highly profitable.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

To generate $1,000,000 in annual recurring revenue, Credex needs:

- $1,000,000 ÷ $5,000 = 200 funded customers per year

With the conversion assumptions above:

- 200 customers require 1,000 consultations
- 1,000 consultations require 10,000 completed audits

Over 18 months, this equals roughly:

- 556 completed audits per month
- 56 consultations per month
- 11 funded customers per month

These targets are realistic if the tool ranks well in communities used by startup engineering and finance leaders.

## Summary

If each completed audit costs approximately $5 to acquire and produces $100 in expected revenue, AI Spend Audit is an attractive lead-generation asset. At modest conversion rates and fewer than 600 audits per month, the tool could support a path to $1M ARR within 18 months.
